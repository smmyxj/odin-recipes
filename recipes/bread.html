Wheat Bread


"Save your tears for the wheat" -- old Draag saying

The country of Draag is known for its beautiful wheat fields. They are a large importer of all types of wheat products including flour and wheat bread. Here is a simple recipe for making your own delicious wheat bread.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Warm water
  • Honey
  • Butter
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Heat Oven to 200 Degrees
  2. Add 3 cups warm water and 1 tbsp of yeast into large mixing bowl and still
  3. Add 2/3 cup of honey and stir again.
  4. Let this proof for five minutes, it will form a creamy foam
  5. Add 1/3 cup of oil, 7 cup of flour, 1 tbsp salt and mix
  6. Keep adding small amounts of flower until the dough pulls away easily from the bowl
  7. Knead by hand for 10 minutes
  8. With small amount of oil in hand, separade the bread into two loaves and add into two loaf pans
  9. Place pans in the warm oven. Let the dough rise until it is doubled in size, about 20 minutes
  10. Turn oven to 350 degrees, leave bread inside
  11. After 25 minutes, check bread. When it is golden brown, remove from oven
  12. Let cool and then remove from pan
  13. Slice
  14. Enjoy!
