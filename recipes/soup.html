Cauliflower Soup


Description

Soup is a popular food in Draag, especially during the winter months. They even advertised their famous soup on the big screens at each of The Black Parade's performances while touring America. Draag soup is famously canned, but you can also make it at home with fresh ingredients. Here is a quick and easy tutorial for making your own Draag inspired soup at home!

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable broth
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Nutmeg
  • Garnish
    • parsley
    • onion
    • chives

Steps

  1. Roast Cauliflower in the oven
  2. Add roasted cauliflower, sauted onions, garlic, and veggie broth to a soup pot
  3. Heat on the stove for 20 minutes
  4. Remove from heat, transfer into a blender and add butter
  5. Blend until creamy and smooth
  6. season by adding lemon juice and nutmeg
  7. pour into bowl and add garnish of choice
  8. Enjoy!
