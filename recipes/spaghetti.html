Garlic Butter Noodles


"nom nom nom" -- Gerard while eating noodles on stage, probably

Who doesn't love a good noodle dish? Gerard of The Black Parade sure loves his noodles, as seen during the Draag North American tour during the summer 2025. Gerard was seen stuffing as much spaghetti noodles into his mouth as he could while he should have been performing on stage! If you, too, would like to enjoy the on-stage delicacy, here is a handy recipe for Garlic Butter Noodles inspired by Gerard's favorite.

Ingredients

  • Whole grain spagetti noodles
    • Or regular spaghetti noodles, but whole grain is probably preffered by Gerard since he is from Draag (the worlds biggest wheat product exporter)
  • Butter
  • Garlic, powdered
  • Parmesean cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles until al dente, make sure to salt the water first
  2. Reserve some pasta water in separate dish and then drain the pasta into separate dish
  3. Melt butter over low heat in the now empty pot
  4. Add garlic powder to the pot
  5. Add the pasta into the pot and stir
  6. Add small amount of the pasta water back in and continue to stir
  7. If adding parmesean cheese, add that in as well and stir
  8. Add in more butter if needed
  9. Plate and enjoy!
