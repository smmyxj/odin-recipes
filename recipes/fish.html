Fish and Chips


"What we are going to decide right now, is what is better: chicken or fish" -- Gerard Way

The illusion of choice is a prominent concept in the country of Draag. And, while they may be the biggest exporter of wheat, they are also known for their chicken and fish. Recently during their tour with The Black Parade, Draag used Chicken and Fish signs in an interactive moment with the crowd. Here is an easy recipe for Draag Fish and Chips!

Ingredients

  • 4 Large russet potatoes
  • Canola oil
  • 3 Cups flour
  • 1 Cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 Tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 Tsp paprika
  • 1/4 Tsp pepper
  • 3 Tsp baking powder
  • 2 Lbs cod fillet, 1 inch in thickness
    • Hake or haddoc acceptable as alternatives
  • 3 Cups beer, any
    • Non-alcoholic beer is also fine

Instructions: Chips Part 1

  1. Peel potatoes and slice off the ends
  2. Cut long to make fry strips
  3. Place into a watr bath (bowl of water) for 30 mins
  4. Remove from water and pat dry
  5. Add two quarts of oil to a dutch oven and heat to 350 degrees
  6. Add fries to hot oil and increase heat
  7. Let fry while stirring
  8. Once fries turn lightly golden and browned at the ends, remove
  9. Let the fries sit on paper towls while excess oil drains from them

Instructions: Fish

  1. Slice fish into perferred portions and pat dry
  2. Whisk the flour to make flour mixture, cornstarch, cayenne, pepper, paprika, and salt in large mixing bowl
  3. In separade flat pan add 1.5 cups of the flour mixture and set aside
  4. In the initial flower mixture add baking powder and mix
  5. Add beer to initial flour mixture, and mix into wet flour mixture
  6. In same dutch oven from the fries, add more oil and heat to 375 degrees
  7. Take each piece of fish and mix in the wet flour mixture
  8. Dip each piece into the dry flour mixture unti covered on all sides and leave laying on the pan
  9. Add each piece into the hot oil in batches
  10. Let the fish fry for around five minutes while stirring
  11. When golden brown, remove from the oil and place aside where excess oil can drain from the chicken

Instructions: Chips Part 2

  1. Add the previously friend potatoes back into the fishy oil for short time, only a couple of minutes
  2. Remove and let the fries drain excess oil again
  3. Sprinkle with salt
Plate the fish and chips, and enjoy!
